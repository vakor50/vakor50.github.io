Cost Splitter

The purpose of this site is to enable a group of friends to split the cost of any number of purchases. For example, if you and some friends go on a trip together and someone buys food for everyone, someone pays for gas, and someone else pays for the hotel room, when you enter these purchases you can select who owes who. THe program then calculates how much each participant owes someone who made a purchase.

Instructions:

  1. Enter the names of the people who participated in the purchase(s).
  2. Enter the information (purchaser, name of purchase, amount) for each purchase made.
  3. Select from the checkboxes which participants of the trip the purchase applies to.
  4. Press "Calculate"
  5. You can now see how much each of your participants owes the people who made the purchase.
